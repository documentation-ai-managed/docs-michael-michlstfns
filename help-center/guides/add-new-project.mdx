---
title: Add a New Project
description: How to add a new project to your documentation space.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open dashboard">
    Log in and go to the Michael / Michlstfns dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Look for the "New Project" button and click on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter your project's name and an optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Press "Create" to add your new project to the space.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">You can add multiple projects to organize your documentation.</Callout>
