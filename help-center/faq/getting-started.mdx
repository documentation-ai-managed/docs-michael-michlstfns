## Getting Started

To create a new documentation space:

1. Sign in to your Michael / Michlstfns account
2. Click "New Project" in the dashboard
3. Enter your project name and description
4. Click "Create"

Your new documentation space is ready. Add pages and customize as needed.

<Callout kind="info">You can manage multiple spaces from your dashboard.</Callout>
