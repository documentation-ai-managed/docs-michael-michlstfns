---
title: Cannot Create New Page
description: Fix issues when new pages can't be created.
---

## Problem
- You click "Add Page" but nothing happens
- No error message is shown

## Possible Causes
- Not signed in
- Lack of permissions

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    Make sure you're signed in to your Michael / Michlstfns account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify permissions">
    Ensure your role allows creating pages (check with your admin).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the browser">
    Sometimes a reload resolves the issue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
